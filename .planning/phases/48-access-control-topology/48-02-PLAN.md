- Loadgen --identity-save to persist owner + delegate keys
- Loadgen --identity-file to reuse saved keys

1. Owner connects to Node1 with --identity-file owner_dir
2. Owner sends delegation blob via --delegate DELEGATE_PUBKEY_HEX --count 1
3. Delegation blob syncs to all nodes in cluster
4. Delegate connects to Node2 with --identity-file delegate_dir, writes blobs
5. Engine on Node2 verifies: delegate's pubkey matches extracted delegate pubkey from delegation blob
