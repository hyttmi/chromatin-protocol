Purpose: Operational flexibility -- operators can reduce or increase peer count limits on a running node. When reduced below current count, excess peers drain naturally (no mass disconnect).

Output: Mutable max_peers_ member, reload_config() support, should_accept_connection and handle_peer_list_response use max_peers_ instead of config_.max_peers.
