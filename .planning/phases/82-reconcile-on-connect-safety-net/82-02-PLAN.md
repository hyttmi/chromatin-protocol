<objective>
Add cursor grace period for disconnected peers and ensure full reconciliation runs on every peer connect, with cursor reuse for quick reconnects.

Purpose: When a peer reconnects after a brief outage (< 5 min), the existing MDBX cursors allow sync to skip namespaces that haven't changed, making reconnect fast. For longer outages, cursors are discarded and full reconciliation starts fresh. This completes the event-driven sync model: push notifications for real-time, reconcile-on-connect for catch-up, safety-net for correctness.

Output: DisconnectedPeerState tracking in PeerManager, grace period logic in connect/disconnect handlers, stale cleanup in safety-net cycle, cursor compaction aware of grace period.
</objective>
