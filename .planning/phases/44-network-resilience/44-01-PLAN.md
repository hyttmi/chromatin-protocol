<objective>
Add auto-reconnect with exponential backoff + jitter for all outbound peers, and ACL-aware reconnection suppression with extended backoff.

Purpose: Without reconnect, disconnected peers are lost until manual restart or PEX rediscovery. Without ACL suppression, rejected peers cause tight retry loops wasting CPU. The handshake_ok bug must be fixed to prevent 1-second retry storms against ACL-rejecting peers.

Output: Server reconnects all outbound peers (bootstrap + discovered) with jittered backoff (1s-60s). ACL-rejecting peers get extended 600s backoff after 3 rejections. SIGHUP resets rejection state.
</objective>
