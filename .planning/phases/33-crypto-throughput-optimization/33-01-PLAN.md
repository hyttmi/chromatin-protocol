<objective>
Implement the hash-then-sign protocol change (PERF-04) and OQS_SIG context caching (PERF-02).

Purpose: These are the two foundational optimizations. PERF-04 eliminates the ~1 MiB intermediate allocation in the signing hot path by replacing raw concatenation with incremental SHA3-256 hashing. PERF-02 eliminates per-call OQS_SIG allocation/deallocation in verify(). Together they remove the two largest constant-factor costs from the crypto pipeline.

Output: `build_signing_input()` returns a 32-byte SHA3-256 digest, `Signer::verify()` uses a cached context, all callers compile, all tests pass.
</objective>
